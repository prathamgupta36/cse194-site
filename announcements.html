Welcome to CSE 194: Cybersecurity History and Culture

ANNOUNCEMENT · WELCOME · 14th January 2026

Hello Hackers,

I am thrilled to start on this exciting journey about cybersecurity history and culture. We will venture into the history of hacking and how everything we now know started, learning more about the hackers who shaped the environment we live in, and have tons of fun along the way. I hope you take away the essence of being a hacker.

This course will go through the good and the evil that gave cybersecurity its modern structure. Ultimately, I hope to have motivated you enough to become a hacker and keep the culture alive.

Happy Hacking and Learning!
